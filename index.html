Hi! I'm a tall, homeschooled, well traveled, terrible speller, who loves ice cream, building things and is hilarious at times.

What's my story?


Well, when I graduated from college, I had no idea what to do with my life. So I founded ProjectONE12, an initiative to redefined the job hunt where I interned at 10 different companies in 112 days across North America (and meet a whole bunch of super awesome people). I ended up in Montreal, Canada to join a startup called PasswordBox as Director of Marketing. My team was amazing and i got to launch a product from beta to reaching millions of users worldwide before being acquired by Intel.

Today, I do all the things. But focus on creative and strategic initiatives for True Key by Intel Security - a cross platform app to remove the hassle of passwords and log in with your face or fingerprint intead. I've been blessed to be nationally recognized for my entrepreneurial work, broadly traveled, built 6 homes in developing countries, written award-nominated academic papers and lead community initiatives to improve the standard of living for aspiring entrepreneurs in Alberta. I am one curiously strange and lucky gal.
