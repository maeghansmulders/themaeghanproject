What's my story?

When I graduated from university I had no idea what to do with my life. So I founded ProjectONE12, an initiative to redefined the job hunt and intern at 10 different companies in 112 days across North America. I ended up in Montreal, Canada and joined PasswordBox, a tech startup as Director of Marketing. I got to launch our product from beta, raise venture capital funding, win best app at CES 2015 and reach millions of users worldwide. After 18 months of crazy adventures, PasswordBox was acquired by Intel.

Today, I do all the things. But focus on creative and strategic initiatives for True Key by Intel Security - a cross platform app to remove the hassle of passwords and log in with your face or fingerprint intead. I've been blessed to be nationally recognized for my entrepreneurial work, broadly traveled, built 6 homes in developing countries, written award-nominated academic papers and lead community projects to improve the standard of living for other entrepreneurs in Alberta.
