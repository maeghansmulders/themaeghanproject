about

Founder of ProjectONE12, Maeghan redefined the job hunt process by interning at 10 different companies in 112 days across North America. She joined Montreal-based startup PasswordBox (acquired by Intel) as Director of Marketing to lead go to market of it's product launch out of beta.

Today, she owns creative and stratgy initiatives for True Key by Intel Security - a cross platform application to remove the hassle of passwords. Maeghan has been nationally recognized for her entrepreneurial work, broadly traveled, built 6 homes in developing countries, written award-nominated academic papers and lead community initiatives to improve the standard of living for aspiring entrepreneurs in Alberta.
